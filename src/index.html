Our Foundation

Odysseus, as made famous by Homer’s epic poem, The Illiad, was one of the greatest heroes in Greek mythology who fought during the Trojan War. Odysseus was known for his cunning intellect and helped devise the plan that conquered the city of Troy and ended the Trojan War. In Homer’s second epic, The Odyssey, Odysseus wanders the seas for ten years, overcoming countless obstacles and challenges as he continues his long journey home.

Enter Odyssey, a cutting edge sports and entertainment marketing company founded by a team of sports enthusiasts and event gurus that live for entertaining the masses and conquering the ongoing sea that is digital marketing. We pride ourselves on our intellect and our ability to adapt to change and any challenge we face. From professional services such as branding and design to creative marketing, Odyssey can deliver the highest quality of event services and new-age digital marketing customized to fit your every need.
